NexaAI Solutions is an enterprise AI company founded in 2021, specializing in AI systems for knowledge management, analytics, and automation. Based in Bengaluru, it serves mid-to-large organizations across technology, finance, healthcare, and education sectors. Their modular product suite includes NexaChat for internal knowledge, NexaInsight for business analytics, NexaSupport for customer automation, and NexaSecure for AI governance. NexaAI aims to simplify knowledge access and automate workflows through practical, reliable, and explainable AI. Pricing plans vary from Starter to Growth and Enterprise, based on users and modules, with annual billing discounts. The company upholds comprehensive policies covering HR, leave (e.g., 18 annual, 10 sick days), professional conduct, and disciplinary actions. Its core values include customer obsession, transparency, accountability, security, and continuous improvement.
